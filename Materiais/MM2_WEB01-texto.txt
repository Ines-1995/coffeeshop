About us
At Aveiro's Coffee House, we offer a wide variety of coffee drinks, including espresso, cappuccino, latte, and more. We also offer a selection of teas, hot chocolate, and other non-coffee beverages.


Events
We love to bring people together! That's why we offer a variety of events throughout the year, including live music, poetry readings, and more. Be sure to check our calendar to stay up-to-date on all of our upcoming events.
